6. Moving Iterator

Forward:

it++;

Backward:

it--;

Example:

auto it = mp.begin();
it++;

Now it points to the second element.

7. Very Important (For Your Recursion Code)

Instead of modifying the same iterator:

❌ Avoid

it++;
solve(mp,ans,temp,it,target);
it--;

✔ Better approach

auto next_it = it;
next_it++;

solve(mp,ans,temp,next_it,target);

This avoids iterator corruption during recursion.
